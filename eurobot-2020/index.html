Eurobot Germany 2020

The Eurobot 2020 will take place this year under the motto “Sail the World”.

This year, the Roboterclub Aachen is the organiser of the German competition, which serves the German teams as a qualification for the international competition in France.

  • When: 01 - 03 May 2020
  • Where: Assembly hall of the RWTH Aachen
  • Who: All German and international teams
  • Registration: Until 29.02.20 via the form.
  • Entry fee: 50€ per team
  • Motto: Sail the World
  • Rules: The rules of Eurobot 2020 apply
  • Audience: Spectators are very welcome

Preliminary Schedule

  • Friday, 1. May
    • From 16:00h: Setup, Test games, Homologation
  • Saturday, 2. May
    • Until noon: Setup, Homologation
    • Lunch
    • Group matches
    • From 22:00h: Evening Event
  • Sunday, 3. May
    • Final rounds
    • Award ceremony
    • From 16:00h: Evening Event
